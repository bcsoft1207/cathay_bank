Our Services

Since 2005, Cathay United Bank has successively established a branch and representative offices in Vietnam, providing diverse financial services for domestic and oversea enterprises there. In 2022, the branch was relocated to Ho Chi Minh City to further develop the Vietnamese market, while the representative office was relocated to Quang Nam province. Cathay United Bank currently has three footholds in Vietnam, namely Ho Chi Minh City Branch, Hanoi Representative Office and Quang Nam Representative Office; where we provide premium banking services spanning across north, central and south Vietnam.
